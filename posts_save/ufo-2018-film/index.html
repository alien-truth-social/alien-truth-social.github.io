UFO (2018) is a science fiction film written and directed by Ryan Eslinger that follows a mathematics student, played by Alex Sharp, as he attempts to prove the reality of extraterrestrial encounters. Assisted by a professor portrayed by Gillian Anderson, the protagonist decodes complex signals to challenge official government cover-ups regarding a UAP sighting. The film offers a grounded, math-centric take on the UFO genre, though critical reception suggests it serves primarily as a modest nod to classic alien-encounter cinema. Sony Pictures released the project directly to video on September 4, 2018.
