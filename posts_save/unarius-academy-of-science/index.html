Unarius Academy of Science

Apr 30, 2026 1 minute read

Founded in 1954 in Los Angeles by Ernest and Ruth Norman, the Unarius Academy of Science is a non-profit organization that promotes an “interdimensional science of life” based on reincarnation and contact with extraterrestrial intelligences. The group, which relocated to El Cajon, California, in 1975, gained public notoriety through its prolific publication of channeled texts and its use of public-access television to promote beliefs in ancient interplanetary civilizations. While the organization prefers to be classified as a scientific educational foundation, sociologists and researchers identify it as a “UFO religion” due to its core tenets, such as the predicted arrival of a space-fleet. Following the deaths of its founders and subsequent leaders, including Charles Louis Spiegel, the group has continued to operate through a council-led structure despite the failure of their specific 2001 millenarian landing prophecy.
