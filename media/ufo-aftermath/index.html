UFO: Aftermath

2003/05/16 · 50 words · 1 minutes to read
Categories: video game
Tags: UFO video game

UFO: Aftermath is a 2003 real-time tactics/turn-based strategy video game created by ALTAR Interactive. It is a homage to the X-COM game series, with roots in the unfinished game The Dreamland Chronicles: Freedom Ridge. It was followed by two sequels, UFO: Aftershock and UFO: Afterlight
