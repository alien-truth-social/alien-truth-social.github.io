Aliens Ate My Homework (2018 film) A 2018 American science fiction comedy film directed by Sean McNamara, based on the novel of the same name by Bruce Coville. The film features an ensemble cast including Jayden Greig, William Shatner, Dan Payne, and Alex Zahara. It premiered on Netflix on March 6, 2018, and was followed by a sequel, Aliens Stole My Body (2020). Plot Rod and his cousin Elspeth encounter a group of extraterrestrial lawmen, the Galactic Patrol, after a spaceship crashes through his window and lands on his homework.

Uncontacted peoples

Categories: Anthropology Film
Tags: Indigenous Peoples Isolation Human Rights
Uncontacted Peoples Uncontacted peoples are Indigenous groups who live without sustained contact with neighboring communities and the world community. They choose to remain isolated, and are referred to as indigenous peoples in voluntary isolation. Estimates suggest there are between 100 and 200 uncontacted tribes, totaling up to 10,000 individuals, primarily located in South America, especially northern Brazil. Knowledge about these groups is mainly gathered from neighboring Indigenous communities and aerial footage.

Pentagon UFO videos

2025/04/23
Categories: Ufology TV Show
Tags: UAP Pentagon Navy
Pentagon UFO videos The Pentagon UFO videos are visual recordings from United States Navy fighter jets, widely publicized as UFOs. These videos were recorded using forward-looking infrared (FLIR) targeting cameras from fighter jets based aboard the aircraft carriers USS Nimitz and USS Theodore Roosevelt in 2004, 2014, and 2015. Additional footage was taken by other Navy personnel in 2019. The videos, characterized as officially documenting UFOs, have received extensive media coverage since 2017.
