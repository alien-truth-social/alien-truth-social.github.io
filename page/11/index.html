1976 Tehran UFO incident

2025/04/03
Categories: Ufology TV Show
Tags: UFO Iran Military
1976 Tehran UFO Incident ðŸ”— The 1976 Tehran UFO incident involved radar and visual sightings of an unidentified flying object (UFO) over Tehran, Iran, in the early morning hours of September 19, 1976. Two Imperial Iranian Air Force (IIAF) F-4 Phantom II jet interceptors were dispatched to investigate. Pilots reported instrumentation and communication failures as they approached the object, which were restored upon withdrawal. One aircraft also experienced a temporary weapons systems failure while preparing to fire.
