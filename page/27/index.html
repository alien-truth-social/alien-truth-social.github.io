Joe Alien ðŸ”— Joe Alien is a British comic book character who appeared in strips published by Fleetway Publications. He debuted in the preview issue of the science fiction anthology Wildcat in October 1988. The character’s strip was one of four linked serials in Wildcat and was later briefly featured in Eagle after Wildcat was canceled. Joe Alien is the last survivor of the planet Xgangbe-4 and joins the Wildcat mission to help humanity find a new home, commanding the Wildcat 2 exploration shuttle.

Categories: History Book
Tags: Dictatorship Torture Political Repression
A Verdade Sufocada ðŸ”— This entry details the book “A Verdade Sufocada” (“The Suffocated Truth”) written by Carlos Alberto Brilhante Ustra, a Brazilian military colonel convicted of torture during Brazil’s military dictatorship (1964-1985). The book presents Ustra’s perspective on the left-wing armed resistance during that period and his experiences as head of DOI-CODI, an agency known for political repression, torture, and murder of the dictatorship’s opponents. The book’s publication faced boycotts, requiring the author’s family to finance its print runs.
