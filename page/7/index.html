Tags: SETI Extraterrestrial Intelligence Radio Astronomy
Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence (SETI) The search for extraterrestrial intelligence (SETI) encompasses various scientific projects aimed at detecting signals or evidence of intelligent life beyond Earth. These efforts involve monitoring electromagnetic radiation, searching for optical signals, and investigating potential extraterrestrial artifacts. Some initiatives, like NASA’s Golden Record, have also attempted to send messages to hypothetical alien civilizations. Despite decades of searching, no confirmed evidence of alien intelligence has been found.

Extraterrestrial Liquid Water Extraterrestrial liquid water refers to water in its liquid state that occurs naturally outside of Earth. Ancient astronauts

2025/04/18
Categories: Pseudoscience Book
Tags: Ancient Astronauts Extraterrestrial Influence
Ancient Astronauts Ancient astronauts, also known as ancient aliens, is a pseudoscientific belief that intelligent extraterrestrial beings visited Earth in ancient times and influenced human civilization. Proponents suggest that these aliens affected the development of cultures, technologies, religions, and even human biology. A common idea is that deities in many religions were actually extraterrestrials, and their advanced technologies were seen as divine powers by early humans. This idea is rejected by academics and archaeologists, who consider it pseudoarchaeology and unscientific.
