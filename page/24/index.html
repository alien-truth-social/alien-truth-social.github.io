Categories: Ufology TV Show
Tags: Ariel School Zimbabwe Sighting
1994 Sighting in Zimbabwe On September 16, 1994, a UFO sighting occurred outside Ruwa, Zimbabwe, witnessed by 62 pupils aged six to twelve at Ariel School. The children reported seeing silver crafts descend from the sky and land in a nearby field. Some claimed to have encountered creatures dressed in black who communicated a telepathic message with an environmental theme, causing fear and distress. Fortean writer Jerome Clark called it the “most remarkable close encounter of the third kind of the 1990s.

Morristown UFO hoax

2025/02/04
Categories: Hoax TV Show
Tags: UFO Skepticism
2009 Morristown UFO Hoax The 2009 Morristown UFO hoax refers to a series of events involving mysterious red lights observed in the sky near Morristown, New Jersey, starting on January 5, 2009. These lights were subsequently seen on four other nights in January and February. The events were later revealed to be a hoax perpetrated by Joe Rudy and Chris Russo, who described it as a social experiment designed to expose the pseudoscience of ufology and highlight the unreliability of eyewitness accounts.
