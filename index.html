Area 51 ðŸ”— Area 51, officially called Homey Airport (KXTA) or Groom Lake, is a highly classified United States Air Force (USAF) facility located within the Nevada Test and Training Range. It’s administered by Edwards Air Force Base. While details of its operations are not public, it’s generally believed to support the development and testing of experimental aircraft and weapons systems. The USAF and CIA acquired the site in 1955, primarily for flight testing the Lockheed U-2 aircraft.

Ancient Astronauts ðŸ”— Ancient astronauts, also known as ancient aliens, is a pseudoscientific belief that intelligent extraterrestrial beings visited Earth in ancient times and influenced the development of human civilization. Proponents suggest that these aliens affected cultures, technologies, religions, and even human biology. A common idea is that deities in various religions were actually extraterrestrials, and their advanced technologies were seen as divine powers by early humans. Academics and archaeologists dismiss these claims as pseudoarchaeology and unscientific, lacking credible peer-reviewed evidence.
