Aliens Ate My Homework (2018 film) ðŸ”—

A 2018 American science fiction comedy film directed by Sean McNamara, based on the novel of the same name by Bruce Coville. The film features an ensemble cast including Jayden Greig, William Shatner, Dan Payne, and Alex Zahara. It premiered on Netflix on March 6, 2018, and was followed by a sequel, Aliens Stole My Body (2020).

Plot ðŸ”—

Rod and his cousin Elspeth encounter a group of extraterrestrial lawmen, the Galactic Patrol, after a spaceship crashes through his window and lands on his homework. They must assist the aliens in stopping the plans of the interplanetary criminal BKR, who is disguised on Earth as Billy Becker, a school bully who torments Rod.

Bruce Coville, the author of the original novel, appears as J. Carter, the principal of Rod’s school, “Coville Elementary.” He has a non-speaking role until a mid-credits scene where he reads his own book and narrates the fate of the villain, BKR.

Release ðŸ”—

Universal Pictures Home Entertainment acquired distribution rights to the film in March 2018, releasing it on March 6, 2018. The film’s estimated domestic DVD sales reached $46,603.
