Development ðŸ”—

Chris Sheridan was inspired by the Resident Alien comic books and a personal UFO sighting. Syfy ordered the pilot in May 2018, with Universal Cable Productions, Dark Horse Entertainment, and Amblin Television developing it. The series was ordered in February 2019, with production in Vancouver and David Dobkin directing the pilot. Robert Duncan McNeill executive produced and directed the remaining episodes.
