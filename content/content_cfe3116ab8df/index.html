Premise ðŸ”—

This section outlines the central plot of the series. An alien crashes on Earth, kills a doctor, and assumes his identity in Patience, Colorado. He is tasked with wiping out humanity but grapples with the morality of his mission, especially after developing compassion and dealing with a child who can see his true form. Production ðŸ”—

This section details the development, casting, and filming aspects of the series.

Development ðŸ”—

Series creator Chris Sheridan was inspired by the Resident Alien comic book series and a personal UFO sighting experience. Syfy announced the TV adaptation in 2018, with Universal Cable Productions, Dark Horse Entertainment, and Amblin Television developing the pilot. Syfy gave a series order in 2019, with production in Vancouver and David Dobkin directing the pilot. Robert Duncan McNeill executive produced and directed the remaining episodes. The series was renewed for a second season in 2021 and a third season in 2022. The episode count for the third season was reduced from 12 to 8. In June 2024, the series was renewed for a fourth season and moved to USA Network.

Casting ðŸ”—

Alan Tudyk was cast as the main character, Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle, along with Sara Tomko, Corey Reynolds, Alice Wetterlund, and Levi Fiehler. Linda Hamilton, Mandell Maughan, and Alex Barima were cast in recurring roles, followed by Elizabeth Bowen.

Filming ðŸ”—

Principal photography for the first season began in September 2020 and concluded in October 2020 in Delta, British Columbia, Canada. Filming for the second season began in August 2021 and concluded in April 2022, with locations including Ladysmith, British Columbia. Filming for the third season began in January 2023 and concluded in May 2023. Filming for the fourth season began on December 2, 2024 and concluded on March 31, 2025.

Release ðŸ”—

Syfy announced the series premiere for summer 2020, later moved to January 2021. The series premiered in Canada on CTV Sci-Fi Channel and in the United Kingdom on Sky One.
