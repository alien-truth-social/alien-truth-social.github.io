Premise ðŸ”—

An alien crash-lands on Earth near a small town in Colorado. To blend in, it kills a vacationing physician named Harry Vanderspeigle and assumes his identity. The alien is tasked with performing an autopsy on the town’s deceased doctor and subsequently takes over his practice. The narrative explores the alien’s internal conflict regarding its mission to wipe out humanity, complicated by developing compassion and moral dilemmas. A key plot point involves the mayor’s son, Max, who can see through the alien’s disguise. As the series progresses, the alien must defend humanity from other extraterrestrial threats.

Series Overview ðŸ”—

The series consists of three seasons, with the first premiering on January 27, 2021, and the third concluding on April 3, 2024. The first season has 10 episodes, the second has 16, and the third has 8. The show originally aired on Syfy, but will move to USA Network for its fourth season.

Season 1 (2021) ðŸ”—

The first season consists of 10 episodes, starting with the “Pilot” directed by David Dobkin. The season follows the alien’s initial crash landing, assumption of Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle’s identity, and struggle to blend in while pursuing its mission. Key events include the discovery of the real Harry’s foot in the lake, the introduction of Max Hawthorne who can see the alien’s true form, and the arrival of the real Harry’s wife, Isabelle. The season culminates with Harry’s decision to spare humanity and his accidental stowaway, Max, on his ship.

Season 2 (2022) ðŸ”—

The second season is divided into two parts, each consisting of eight episodes. The season explores Harry’s attempts to prevent an alien invasion, his journey to New York, and the emergence of Goliath, his future self. Key events include Harry losing his memory, Asta discovering she has a daughter, and the introduction of new characters like Carlyn and Violinda Darvell. The season ends with Harry learning about Grey-human hybrids and the abduction of Ben Hawthorne.

Season 3 (2024) ðŸ”—

The third season consists of 8 episodes. Harry works with General McCallister to eliminate the Grey Alien threat. Asta and Harry work together to undermine Joseph, a Grey-human hybrid. Ben and Kate experience troubling visions related to their abductions. Harry has a romantic relationship with a Blue Avian alien named Heather. The season culminates with Harry’s bomb going missing, D’arcy taking the bomb to the Greys’ base, and the revelation that the Greys’ mother ship is actually Earth’s Moon.

Production ðŸ”—

Development ðŸ”—

Chris Sheridan was inspired by the Resident Alien comic book series and a personal “close encounter” experience. Syfy announced the TV adaptation in May 2018, with Sheridan as the show creator. The series was officially ordered in February 2019, with production based in Vancouver. David Dobkin directed the pilot, and Robert Duncan McNeill served as an executive producer and producing director. The series was renewed for a second season in March 2021 and a third season in July 2022. In June 2024, it was announced that the series was renewed for a fourth season and moving to the USA Network.

Casting ðŸ”—

Alan Tudyk was cast as the main character in September 2018, along with Sara Tomko, Corey Reynolds, Alice Wetterlund, and Levi Fiehler. Linda Hamilton, Mandell Maughan, and Alex Barima were cast in recurring roles in January 2020, followed by Elizabeth Bowen in February 2020.

Filming ðŸ”—

Filming for the first season began in September 2020 and concluded in October 2020 in Delta, British Columbia, Canada. The second season was filmed between August 2021 and April 2022, with locations including Ladysmith, British Columbia. Filming for the third season began in January 2023 and concluded in May 2023. Filming for the fourth season began on December 2, 2024 and concluded on March 31, 2025.

Release ðŸ”—

Syfy initially announced a summer 2020 premiere, which was later moved to January 2021. The series premiered on January 27, 2021, in the United States and Canada, and on Sky One in the United Kingdom the following day.
