Premise ðŸ”—

This section outlines the central plot of the series. An alien arrives on Earth with the mission to wipe out humanity. After crashing in Colorado, the alien assumes the identity of a doctor named Harry Vanderspeigle. As he lives among humans, he begins to question his mission. A young boy, Max Hawthorne, can see through his disguise. The alien grapples with his mission and ultimately defends humanity from other extraterrestrial threats.

Cast and characters ðŸ”—

This section details the main and recurring cast members and their respective characters.

Main ðŸ”—

  • Alan Tudyk plays the alien, who takes on the identity of Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle. He is sent to Earth to destroy humanity but develops compassion. Tudyk also portrays the real Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle.
  • Sara Tomko plays Asta Twelvetrees, the assistant to the town doctor and a member of the Ute tribe. She is the first person after Max to discover Harry’s true identity.
  • Corey Reynolds plays Sheriff Mike Thompson, the town’s sheriff, who is dealing with past trauma.
  • Alice Wetterlund plays D’arcy Bloom, the owner of the town pub and Asta’s best friend. She discovers Harry’s true identity after learning that the real Harry killed Sam Hodges.
  • Levi Fiehler plays Ben Hawthorne, the town’s mayor, who has a history of alien abductions.
  • Judah Prehn plays Max Hawthorne, Ben’s son, who can see Harry’s alien form.
  • Elizabeth Bowen plays Deputy Liv Baker, the town’s deputy, who is fascinated by extraterrestrials.
