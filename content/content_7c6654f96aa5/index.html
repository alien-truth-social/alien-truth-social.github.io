Alleged humanoid extraterrestrials ðŸ”—

This entry focuses on “Nordics,” “Nordic aliens,” or “Tall Whites,” which are names given in ufology to humanoid races purportedly from the Pleiades star cluster. These beings are described as resembling Nordic, Germanic, or Scandinavian humans, with features like tall stature (6-7 ft), blond hair, blue eyes, and fair skin. Some accounts mention pale-blue/grey or pastel purple skin tones. George Adamski is noted as one of the first to report contact with these beings in the 1950s. The mythology surrounding them often includes telepathy, benevolence, and physical beauty.

History ðŸ”—

This section discusses the historical context and cultural influences related to the Nordic alien phenomenon. David J. Skal suggests that the 1951 film The Day the Earth Stood Still may have inspired early stories. Stephanie Kelley-Romano describes alien abduction beliefs as a “living myth,” associating Nordic aliens with spiritual growth and protection. These aliens are often portrayed as benevolent beings concerned with Earth’s ecology and world peace, possessing telepathic powers and described as paternal and youthful. While popular in the 1950s, claims of encounters became less common as the grey alien archetype rose to prominence.

Publications by those claiming to have been contacted ðŸ”—

This section lists books written by individuals who claim to have had personal contact with Nordic aliens. These include Flying Saucers Have Landed and Inside the Space Ships by George Adamski, From Outer Space to You by Howard Menger, The Walton Experience by Travis Walton, and Millennial Hospitality by Charles James Hall (adapted into the film Walking with the Tall Whites). The UFO religion Universe People also publishes interactions as “Talks with Teachings from my Cosmic Friends.”
