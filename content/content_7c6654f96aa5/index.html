Nordic Aliens ðŸ”—

In ufology, “Nordics,” “Nordic aliens,” or “Tall Whites” are terms used to describe alleged humanoid extraterrestrial races from the Pleiades star cluster. These beings are said to resemble Nordic, Germanic, or Scandinavian humans, with features like tall stature (6-7 ft), blond hair, blue eyes, and fair skin. Some reports describe their skin tone as pale blue/grey or pastel purple.

History ðŸ”—

Cultural historian David J. Skal suggests that early stories of Nordic aliens may have been inspired by the 1951 film The Day the Earth Stood Still. Stephanie Kelley-Romano describes alien abduction beliefs as a “living myth,” noting that believers often associate Nordic aliens with spiritual growth and protection.

In ufology, Nordic aliens are often portrayed as benevolent beings concerned about Earth’s ecology and world peace, possessing telepathic powers and described as paternal, watchful, smiling, affectionate, and youthful.

During the 1950s, many people, especially in Europe, claimed encounters with these beings. However, such claims became less common in later decades as the grey alien became more prevalent in extraterrestrial encounter accounts.

Publications by Those Claiming to Have Been Contacted ðŸ”—

Several books detail alleged personal contact with Nordic aliens, including:

  • Flying Saucers Have Landed by Desmond Leslie and George Adamski
  • Inside the Space Ships by George Adamski
  • From Outer Space to You by Howard Menger
  • The Walton Experience by Travis Walton
  • Millennial Hospitality by Charles James Hall (adapted into the film Walking with the Tall Whites)

The UFO religion Universe People also publishes interactions with “Cosmic Friends.”
