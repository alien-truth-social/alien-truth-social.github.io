This section introduces the concept of the vast number of extraterrestrial races in the DC Comics universe, noting that the majority are humanoid in structure.

United Planets ðŸ”—

This section discusses the United Planets, a governing body active from the 21st to the 31st centuries. It mentions known members like planets of the Solar System, Htrae, buffer planets seeded by Mon-El, and neighboring empires. It also notes that in the 21st century, the United Planets are led by the corrupt Durlan Thaaros. The section is marked as needing expansion.

Adaptoids ðŸ”—

This section describes the Adaptoids, blob-like creatures from the vicinity of Arcturus who attempted to wipe out the human race on Earth-One. They were defeated by the Justice League when exposed to the Thanagarian equalizing disease.

Aellans ðŸ”—

This section details the Aellans, yellow-skinned reptilian humanoids from Aello, Demonia, and Harpis. Harpis and Doc of Aello were members of the Omega Men.

Alien Invaders ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Alien Invaders as the nameless primary antagonists of Armageddon: The Alien Agenda.

Almeraci ðŸ”—

This section describes the Almeraci, a humanoid race of technologically advanced warriors with psionic abilities, ruled by the matriarchal House of the Blood Royale. Key figures include Maxima, Queen of Almerac, her handmaiden Sazu, and Ultraa.

Aloi ðŸ”—

This section details the Aloi, blue-skinned aliens with ridged foreheads at war with the Margoi over a device called the “Grayl.” Tumlat represents them.

Alstairans ðŸ”—

This section describes the Alstairans of Alstair, plantlike humanoids with leaves instead of hair, constantly at war with other races in the Antares system. Their ruler is Hyathis, an alien plant elemental.

Anasazi ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Anasazi as metahumans resettled to the planet Starhaven, represented by Dawnstar.

Andromedans ðŸ”—

This section details the Andromedans of the Andromeda Galaxy, a race of superhumans similar to Kryptonians and Daxamites, with red hair, albino skin, and blue eyes. Karb-Brak, an Andromedan exiled to Earth, is mentioned.

Angtuans ðŸ”—

This section describes the Angtuans, inhabitants of the planet Angtu, which was destroyed by the mutated Mano in the 30th century.

Anunnake ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Anunnake as reptilian aliens who claim to have killed off the dinosaurs, also known as the Hunter/Gatherers.

Anndrann ðŸ”—

This section details the Anndrann, green-skinned military geniuses from Anndranna, famed for their weapons of war. Iron has a negative effect on their cognitive abilities.

Appellaxians ðŸ”—

This section describes the Appellaxians of Appellax, technologically advanced energy aliens with the ability to create powerful artificial host bodies. The seven Appellaxians that invaded created bodies named Golden Roc, Wood King, Stone-God, Crystal Creature, Fire-Lord, Glass-Man, and Mercury Monster. Their invasion led to the formation of the Justice League. The section also mentions their appearance in Young Justice, where Bruno Mannheim combines them into a Golem.

Aipitons ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Aipitons as small flying insects from the planet Apaiton, represented by the deceased Green Lantern Bzzd.

Aquoids ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Aquoids as natives of planet Beltair IV.

Aranes ðŸ”—

This section details the Aranes of Arane II, light blue-skinned humanoids with tiny blue horns. Rolind Siepur of Arane II is a member of the Dark Circle.

Ardenans ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Ardenans as orange scaly-skinned humanoids from the planet Arden, represented by the deceased Green Lanterns Lodar and Meeno Monak.

Astonians ðŸ”—

This section describes the Astonians, a minor alien race saved from extinction by the Blue Lantern Saint Walker, who is of this race.

Azarathians ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Azarathians as people belonging to the parallel universe of Azarath. Raven is an Azarathian.

This section lists alien races whose names begin with the letter “B.”

Barrions ðŸ”—

This section details the Barrions of Barrio III, silicon-based lifeforms represented by the Green Lantern Chaselon.

Bellatrix Bombers ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Bellatrix Bombers as alien warlords featured in Green Lantern (vol. 3) #20.

Bgztlians ðŸ”—

This section describes the Bgztlians, humans from the colony Bgztl who possess the metahuman ability of intangibility. Phantom Girl of the Legion of Super-Heroes represents them.

Blight ðŸ”—

This section details the Blight, a cybernetic race with rotting flesh, villains of the Legion of Super-Heroes. Their leader is Atrophos, who sought immortality from M’onel.

Bloodline Parasites ðŸ”—

This section describes the Bloodline Parasites, extraterrestrial lifeforms capable of activating the human metagene while feeding on spinal fluid. They have exoskeletons, strength, and the ability to duplicate human form.

Braalians ðŸ”—

This section details the Braalians, humans from the colony Braal who possess the metahuman ability of electromagnetic field manipulation. Cosmic Boy and Magno of the Legion of Super-Heroes represent them.

Brain-Parasites of Planet X ðŸ”—

This section describes the Brain-Parasites of Planet X, who steal knowledge from other races using Knowledge-Extractors.

Branx ðŸ”—

This section details the Branx of Emana Branx, gray-skinned, four-armed warriors who serve the Citadelians. They are native to the Vega star system.

Brylyx ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Brylyx as white-skinned aliens featured in Green Lantern: Mosaic.

The Bolovax Vikians ðŸ”—

This section describes the Bolovax Vikians, a highly communal and technologically advanced race. Kilowog of the Green Lantern Corps is the last surviving member. The section also mentions a holographic Bolovax Vikian appearing in My Adventures with Superman.

Bugs of New Genesis ðŸ”—

This section details the Bugs of New Genesis, an insectoid race spread on New Genesis during their war with Apokolips. Forager and Mantis are prominent members.

This section lists alien races whose names begin with the letter “C.”

Cairnians ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Cairnians as members of L.E.G.I.O.N.

Calatonians ðŸ”—

This section describes the Calatonians, an ancient monarchy on the planet Calaton ravaged by Doomsday.

Canopians ðŸ”—

This section identifies the Canopians as members of Atari Force. Canopians such as Morphea are from New Earth in a parallel universe.

Carggites ðŸ”—

This section describes the Carggites, humans from the colony Cargg who possess the metahuman ability of duplication. Triplicate Girl of the Legion of Super-Heroes represents them.

Catherix ðŸ”—

This section details the Cathexis, alien super-scientists from the “sixth dimension” who created self-aware reality-endangering technology.

Changralynians ðŸ”—

This section describes the Changralynians of Changralyn, silicon-based pacifists represented by Broot of the Omega Men.
