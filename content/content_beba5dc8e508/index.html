The Allianceâ€“Union universe is a fictional setting created by C. J. Cherryh for her science fiction novels. It spans the third and fourth millennia and focuses on humanity’s expansion into space. The universe is dominated by three major powers: Earth Company, the Alliance, and the Union. The Earth Company’s control is challenged by the Alliance and the Union, leading to conflicts and wars. The universe also includes various alien species and explores themes of cultural identity, political maneuvering, and technological advancement.

This section details the historical timeline of the Allianceâ€“Union universe. It begins with humanity’s initial expansion into space, financed by the Sol Corporation (later Earth Company). The development of space stations and the emergence of distinct cultural identities among stationers and merchanters are discussed. The discovery of Pell’s World and its native species, the Hisa, disrupts the existing power balance. The development of faster-than-light (FTL) travel leads to Earth’s attempts to reassert control, resulting in unrest and the formation of the Union. Conrad Mazian’s rebellion and Signy Mallory’s defection are mentioned, along with the eventual establishment of a peace treaty and the rise of tensions between the Alliance and the Union.
