This article documents alleged UFO sightings in Belgium, focusing primarily on the significant wave of sightings between 1989 and 1990.

1989-1990 ðŸ”—

This section highlights the Belgian UFO wave, a period of intense UFO activity. The main feature of this wave was the frequent reports of triangular UFOs observed across the country. These objects were even pursued by Belgian Air Force F-16 fighter jets, and some photographs were taken. One speculative explanation suggested these sightings might have been misidentified stealth aircraft, specifically the F-117 Nighthawk, using Belgian highways for navigation.

1990-03-30 ðŸ”—

This section details a specific mass sighting event that occurred on March 30, 1990, in Ans, Wallonia. Numerous witnesses reported seeing large, silent, low-flying black triangles. These objects were tracked by multiple NATO radar systems and intercepted by jet fighters. The Belgian military investigated the incident, and photographic evidence is said to exist. A close encounter was also reported during this event.
