My Guardian Alien ðŸ”—

A 2024 Philippine television drama series broadcast by GMA Network. It is a science fiction drama directed by Zig Madamba Dulay, starring Marian Rivera. The series premiered on April 1, 2024, and concluded on June 28, 2024, with 65 episodes. Originally titled Against All Odds, it was later renamed. The series is also available for streaming on YouTube.

Premise ðŸ”—

The plot centers around Katherine, who dies in a shooting accident. During her funeral, an alien “pod” lands on Earth, and the alien takes Katherine’s form.
