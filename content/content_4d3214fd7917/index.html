Alien Hive ðŸ”—

Alien Hive is a puzzle video game developed and published by Malaysia-based studio Appxplore in March 2013. It is available on iOS, Android, and Windows Phone platforms. The game blends match-three mechanics with sliding puzzle elements.

Gameplay ðŸ”—

The core gameplay involves swiping tiles to match eggs and aliens, with the goal of hatching, breeding, and evolving them into the Supreme Alien. Players aim to build the ultimate alien hive by matching resource tiles while avoiding Naughty Bots. Power-ups can be crafted to eliminate these bots, and players are encouraged to minimize the number of moves used. As players progress, they encounter more aliens and uncover their mysteries. Magic Fruit, collected by merging seeds into saplings, is crucial for evolving Supreme Aliens, allowing players to merge similar-level aliens using only two of a kind instead of three. Evolved Supreme Aliens can be activated using gold to provide special bonuses in future games.

In App Purchase ðŸ”—

Alien Hive incorporates microtransactions, where real-world money can be exchanged for in-game gold to purchase consumable items. This aspect of the game has faced criticism, with one review suggesting that microtransactions are a necessity.

Reception ðŸ”—

Alien Hive received an average score of 70 out of 100 based on reviews aggregated by Metacritic. By 2015, the game had achieved over 3 million downloads across all platforms.
