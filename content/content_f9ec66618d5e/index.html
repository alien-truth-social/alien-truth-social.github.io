Alien TV is a live-action/CG animation television series that premiered on 9Go! in Australia on November 23, 2019, and on Netflix. The show was created by Ian Brown and developed by Doug Hadders and Adam Rotstein. The series follows a television news crew of extraterrestrials named Ixbee, Pixbee, and Squee, who are sent to Earth to learn about the planet.

Cast ðŸ”—

The main voice cast includes Rob Tinkler as Ixbee, Julie Lemieux as Pixbee, John Cleland as Squee, Kyle Dooley as Squood, and Rupert Degas as the Translator.

Episodes ðŸ”—

The series consists of two seasons. Season 1 was released on August 21, 2020, and Season 2 was released on March 19, 2021. There are no plans for a season 3.

The series is divided into two seasons, with 13 episodes each. Season 1 was released in 2020, and Season 2 in 2021. Each episode contains three segments.

Season 1 consists of 13 episodes, each containing three segments. Each segment focuses on the aliens’ experiences with different aspects of Earth culture and activities, such as bicycles, gymnastics, camping, playgrounds, art, gum, gyms, birthdays, restaurants, BBQs, offices, Halloween, pizza, magic, cleaning, arcades, dumplings, presents, junkyards, spas, love, movies, kindergarten, sickness, babies, supermarkets, tea, weddings, Loch Ness, soccer, skateboards, mannequins, survival, toyshops, laundromats, yoga, Santa, trophies, and makeup. Each segment is directed by Ian Brown and written by various writers, including Doug Hadders, Adam Rotstein, Jeff Sager, Brian Hartigan, Shawn Kalb, Cleon Prineas, Laurie Elliott, Bruce Griffiths, Simon Dodd, Jennifer Daley, Thomas Duncan-Watt and Naomi Jardine.
