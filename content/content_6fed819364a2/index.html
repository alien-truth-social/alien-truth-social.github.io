Notable Sightings ðŸ”—

This section lists various notable UFO sightings and incidents, categorized chronologically and geographically. Some key events include:

  • 1947 Wave: The surge of UFO sightings in 1947, marking the beginning of the modern UFO era.
  • 1952 Flap: A series of UFO sightings over Washington, D.C.
  • Barney and Betty Hill Incident: A famous alleged alien abduction case.
  • Belgian Wave: A series of UFO sightings over Belgium in the late 1980s and early 1990s.
  • Calvine Photo: A controversial UFO photograph from Scotland.
  • Chiles-Whitted Encounter: A 1948 sighting by commercial pilots.
  • Flatwoods Monster: A West Virginia sighting of a strange creature.
  • Gorman Dogfight: A 1948 encounter between a pilot and a UFO.
  • Kenneth Arnold Sighting: The event that popularized the term “flying saucer.”
  • Mantell Crash: The death of a pilot while pursuing a UFO.
  • Mariana Film: A 1950 UFO sighting filmed in Montana.
  • McMinnville Photos: A set of photographs purportedly showing a UFO.
  • Rendlesham Forest Incident: A series of reported sightings near a US military base in the UK.
  • Roswell Incident: The alleged crash of a UFO in Roswell, New Mexico.
  • Sightings in Outer Space: UFO sightings reported by astronauts.
  • “Swamp Gas” Reports: UFO reports attributed to natural phenomena.
  • Pentagon UFO Videos: Declassified videos of UFOs recorded by US Navy pilots.
  • Phoenix Lights: A mass sighting of lights over Phoenix, Arizona.
