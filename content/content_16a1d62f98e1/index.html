  • Leslie Kean: An investigative journalist and author who is notable for books about UFOs and the afterlife.
  • John Keel: A journalist who investigated the Mothman sightings in West Virginia.
  • Donald Keyhoe: An aviator and Marine Corps officer, was the leader of NICAP.
  • Philip J. Klass: Senior editor of Aviation Week and Space Technology and a leading UFO skeptic/debunker.
  • George Knapp: An American investigative journalist.
  • Kevin H. Knuth: An associate professor of physics at University at Albany, author of scientific papers based on US Navy personnel UFO observations.
  • Bob Lazar: Claims to have worked at an area called S-4.
  • Avi Loeb: An Israeli-American theoretical physicist who works on astrophysics and cosmology, Professor of Science at Harvard University.
  • Bruce Maccabee: A retired US Navy optical physicist who has analyzed numerous UFO videos and photos.
  • John E. Mack: A Harvard psychiatrist/professor and alien abduction researcher.
  • James E. McDonald: A physicist and professor of meteorology at the University of Arizona.
  • Jim Marrs: A conspiracy theorist, news reporter, college professor, and author.
  • Riley Martin: A self-described alien contactee, author, and radio host.
  • Donald Howard Menzel: A professor of astronomy at Harvard University and a leading UFO skeptic.
  • James W. Moseley: Editor of Saucer Smear, long-time observer, author and commentator of the UFO phenomena.
  • Linda Moulton Howe: A journalist known for investigating cattle mutilations.
