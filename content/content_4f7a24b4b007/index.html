Plot ðŸ”—

The plot revolves around Ted Angelo, an alcoholic writer who discovers a 16th-century Spanish ship inside a mountain cave in Cartagena, Colombia. He learns the cave is actually the inside of a crashed UFO. He is pursued by hitmen, a Nazi fugitive named Heinrich Holzmann, and CIA operatives. He teams up with art historian June and eventually involves his ex-wife Maureen. Maureen is revealed to be an alien who has been disguising herself as a human and manipulating world events. She is killed by June. Ted and June go into hiding with an indigenous tribe, where Ted continues to write about his experiences.

This section summarizes critical reception of the film. Sci-Fi 100 noted the initial lack of science fiction elements but found the last 30 minutes more engaging. Film Authority described the film as a mix of Romancing the Stone, Close Encounters of the Third Kind, and The Terminator.
