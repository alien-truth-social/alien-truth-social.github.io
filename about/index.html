About Us

Welcome to Alien Truth Social! Here, your privacy and browsing experience are paramount. We’re committed to providing valuable content while respecting your personal space online.

Your Privacy Matters

  • No Cookies: We don’t use any cookies. This means no tracking of your activity on our site or anywhere else on the internet. You can browse freely without the concern of being followed.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We have no interest in your name, email, or any other personal details. Your usage of our site remains anonymous.
  • Anonymous Visits: Your IP address and other identifying information are not logged or stored. Your visit here is private.
  • Limited & Respectful Advertising: To support the costs of running this website, we display advertisements. However, we are committed to making these as unobtrusive as possible:
    • Contextual Ads Only: The ads you see are related to the content of the page you are currently viewing, not based on your browsing history or personal profile.
    • Least Intrusive Formats: We strive to use ad formats that are less disruptive to your reading experience. We aim to avoid pop-ups, auto-playing videos, and other highly annoying ad types.

Our Approach to Sustainability

We believe it’s possible to fund a website through advertising without compromising your privacy. By focusing solely on contextual ads, we can show relevant information without needing to track your online behavior. Our goal is to find a balance that allows us to continue providing valuable content while respecting your right to privacy and a pleasant browsing experience.

What You Can Expect

  • Privacy-Focused Experience: Browse our content knowing your personal data is not being collected or used.
  • Relevant, Not Personal, Ads: Any advertisements you see will be related to the topic you’re interested in at that moment.
  • Minimal Disruption: We are dedicated to using ad formats that interfere with your reading as little as possible.

Thank you for visiting Alien Truth Social. We appreciate you choosing a platform that values your privacy.
