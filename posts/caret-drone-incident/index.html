"CARET" Drone Incident

The "CARET" Drone Incident involved alleged sightings and photographs of small, unidentified aircraft in California and Lake Tahoe, Nevada, between May and June 2007.

May 18, 2026 1 minute read

The “CARET” Drone Incident involved alleged sightings and photographs of small, unidentified aircraft in California and Lake Tahoe, Nevada, between May and June 2007. These sightings gained significant media attention, with some calling the photographs the clearest UFO images since the Gulf Breeze incident. However, in 2023, an artist claimed to have created the “alien language” and 3D models used in the incident, stating it was a hoax orchestrated for monetary gain. Investigations by VFX artists and others suggested the photos were computer-generated, and later research located where the photos were actually taken, contradicting the original claims.
