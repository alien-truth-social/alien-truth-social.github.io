The Coming of the Saucers

_The Coming of the Saucers_ is a 1952 book by Kenneth Arnold and Raymond Palmer that expands on their earlier articles about unidentified flying objects.

Oct 27, 2024 1 minute read

The Coming of the Saucers is a 1952 book by Kenneth Arnold and Raymond Palmer that expands on their earlier articles about unidentified flying objects. The book includes Arnold’s firsthand accounts of his sighting of flying disc-like shapes and his investigations into various UFO incidents. It also features third-person summaries of other UFO reports and is noted for introducing the concept of the “man in black.” The work has been cited as fueling the extraterrestrial hypothesis and influencing later UFO conspiracy theories.
