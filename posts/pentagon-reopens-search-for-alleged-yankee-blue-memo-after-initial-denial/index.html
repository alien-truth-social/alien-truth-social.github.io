Pentagon Reopens Search for Alleged ‘Yankee Blue’ Memo After Initial Denial

A FOIA appellate decision has ordered the Department of War to conduct additional searches for a 2023 memorandum.

Dec 15, 2025 1 minute read

A FOIA appellate decision has ordered the Department of War to conduct additional searches for a 2023 memorandum. This memorandum was reportedly issued by the Secretary of Defense’s office to halt an Air Force hazing ritual called “Yankee Blue,” which involved fabricated claims of reverse-engineering non-human technology. Initially, the Department of War stated no such memorandum was found, but the appeal has now been granted, requiring a new search. The original Wall Street Journal article described the alleged directive but did not publish the memo or provide specific sourcing.
