Pentagon Releases Files Related to Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP)

The Pentagon has begun releasing government files concerning unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAP), also known as UFOs.

May 08, 2026 1 minute read

The Pentagon has begun releasing government files concerning unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAP), also known as UFOs. These files include FBI reports on unexplained sightings and NASA debriefs with astronauts who observed unidentified objects on the moon. Images released alongside the files depict various phenomena, including an object seen by a U.S. military operator over the Middle East in May 2022 and a football-shaped UAP reported near Japan in 2024. Archival photographs from the Apollo 12 and Apollo 17 missions also show unidentified phenomena on the lunar surface.
