Project Grudge was a U.

Apr 16, 2026 1 minute read

Project Grudge was a U.S. Air Force project that investigated UFOs from February to December 1949. Its stated goal was to alleviate public anxiety by explaining UFO sightings as conventional phenomena, mass hysteria, hoaxes, or psychopathological individuals. The project’s sole formal report concluded there was no evidence of foreign development and recommended reducing the scope of UFO investigations. Critics like J. Allen Hynek and Edward J. Ruppelt argued Project Grudge was more of a public relations campaign than an objective scientific examination.

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_Grudge
